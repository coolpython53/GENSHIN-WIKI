# Brilliant Chrysanthemum

Brilliant Chrysanthemum is a Local Specialty found in Natlan.

## How to obtain
Brilliant Chrysanthemums can be found near water around Huitztli Hill, the Stadium of the Sacred Flame, and the west side of Ochkanatlan.

## Shops

There are 2 shops that sell Brilliant Chrysanthemum:

- Kuzmich (Cost: 1,000 {[Mora]page::Currencies/Mora}) 10 in stock
- Motolina (Cost: 1,000 {[Mora]page::Currencies/Mora}) 5 in stock
  - Refreshes every 3 days

## Crafting
No crafting recipes use Brilliant Chrysanthemum.

## Ascension
1 character uses Brilliant Chrysanthemum for ascension:
- Xi­lo­nen

## Description
A flower with a shape like floating wings. Its petals sway in an unbelievably relaxed, serene manner as they are caressed by the wind.
Those individuals that sort through woven scrolls have conducted research on this flower, and noted that since it was first recorded, its shape has not changed in the slightest. It is said that long ago, plants would covertly transform themselves by mimicking the shapes of animals. Perhaps then, by observing its current form, we may glimpse an impression of the creatures that once roamed this very land.
