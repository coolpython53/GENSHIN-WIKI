# Calla Lily

image::https://raw.githubusercontent.com/coolpython53/GENSHIN-WIKI/main/pages/Items/Materials/Local_Specialties/Calla_Lily/blueprint.jpg::::25

Calla Lilies are a Local Specialty found in the wild all over Mondstadt.

## How to Obtain
Calla Lilies grow around water bodies throughout Mondstadt; many can notably be found around the Teleport Waypoint south of Springvale.

## Shops
There is 1 Shop that sells Calla Lily:
- Flora (Cost: 1,000 {[Mora]page::Currencies/Mora}) 5 in stock
  - Refreshes every 3 days

## Description
A flower that grows near water sources. When cooked, the petals have a chunky texture, yet are sweet and a little bitter. Modern medicine recognizes Calla Lily's antidotal properties, but its astringent flavor is repulsive to many people.
