# Item
Items in Genshin Impact are individual objects or units that players can obtain. Most items can be accessed from the Inventory or Archive, while some others can only be seen in Menus.

## Inventory Categories
- Weapons - Equipment used by characters to attack. Includes swords, claymores, polearms, catalysts and bows.
  - Weapon Enhancement Materials - Items that provide Weapon EXP used to enhance Weapons.
- Artifacts - Equipment used to increase the attributes or stats of characters.
  - Artifact Enhancement Materials - Items that provide Artifact EXP used to enhance Artifacts.
- Character Development Items - Items used to upgrade characters and weapons.
- Food - Items that can be consumed to heal, revive and buff characters or the party.
  - Potions - Items that can add buffs to the Party.
- Materials - Items found in the world used to upgrade characters or create other items.
- Gadgets - Generally reusable tools used by the player to interact with the world, along with some mementos and special items.
- Quest Items - Items obtained during Quests to keep or submit elsewhere.
- Precious Items - Rare items, which includes keepsakes, special consumables, items that serve special functions, and items that can be exchanged or submitted elsewhere.
- Furnishings - Objects that can be placed in the Serenitea Pot.
