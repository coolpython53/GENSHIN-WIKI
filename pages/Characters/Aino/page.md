# Aino
image::https://raw.githubusercontent.com/coolpython53/GENSHIN-WIKI/main/pages/Characters/Aino/aino.webp::::25

Aino is a playable Hydro character in Genshin Impact.

She is a child prodigy and the founder of the Clink-Clank Krumkake Craftshop who created Ineffa and many other intricate machinery. She is an orphan and sees her creations and screws as her "family."

After reaching Friendship Level 6 with Aino, a Krumkake Bolt may be obtained through the character menu.
